Here’s a well-structured list of **Hadoop Engineer interview questions**—categorized from basic to advanced, including scenario-based and practical topics.

***

# 🔹 1. Basic Hadoop Interview Questions

*   What is Hadoop? Explain its core components.
*   What are the main modules of Hadoop ecosystem?
*   What is HDFS? How does it work?
*   What is the difference between Hadoop 1.x and Hadoop 2.x?
*   What are NameNode and DataNode?
*   What is block size in HDFS? Default value?
*   What is replication factor? Why is it needed?
*   What happens when a DataNode fails?
*   What is rack awareness?
*   What are Hadoop daemons?

***

# 🔹 2. HDFS Deep-Dive Questions

*   How does HDFS read/write process work?
*   Explain the architecture of HDFS.
*   What is FSImage and EditLog?
*   What is Secondary NameNode? Is it a backup?
*   What is checkpointing?
*   What is balancing in HDFS?
*   What is safe mode?
*   How is data consistency maintained in HDFS?

***

# 🔹 3. MapReduce Questions

*   What is MapReduce? Explain its phases.
*   What is the role of Mapper and Reducer?
*   What is Combiner? When to use it?
*   What is Partitioner?
*   What happens if there are no reducers?
*   Difference between MapReduce v1 and YARN.
*   How does shuffling and sorting work?
*   What is speculative execution?
*   How do you optimize MapReduce jobs?

***

# 🔹 4. YARN Questions

*   What is YARN? Why was it introduced?
*   Components of YARN architecture (ResourceManager, NodeManager, etc.)
*   What is ApplicationMaster?
*   What are containers in YARN?
*   Difference between YARN and MapReduce?
*   Scheduler types (FIFO, Fair, Capacity Scheduler)
*   How does resource allocation work in YARN?

***

# 🔹 5. Hive Questions

*   What is Hive? How does it differ from RDBMS?
*   Explain Hive architecture.
*   What are managed vs external tables?
*   What is partitioning and bucketing?
*   What is Hive Metastore?
*   Difference: Hive vs Pig vs SQL.
*   What file formats does Hive support?
*   What is ORC/Parquet?
*   How does Hive query execution work?

# 🔹 6. Spark vs Hadoop (Very Important)

*   Why Spark is faster than Hadoop?
*   What is RDD?

*   What is lazy evaluation?
*   When would you use Hadoop over Spark?
